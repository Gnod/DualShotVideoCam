Features 功能

Everything you need. 一切所需。

Professional dual-camera recording, designed for creators. 为创作者设计的专业双摄录制工具。

01

Dual Recording 双摄同步

Wide-angle portrait and ultra-wide landscape simultaneously using AVCaptureMultiCamSession. 利用 AVCaptureMultiCamSession 同时录制广角竖屏和超广角横屏。

02

Split View 分屏预览

Vertically stacked preview with full visibility of both camera feeds. 上下分屏同时预览两个画面，两个视角一目了然。

03

Picture in Picture 画中画

Full-screen wide preview with draggable ultra-wide floating window. 广角全屏预览，超广角可拖拽浮窗叠加显示。

04

Pro Quality 专业画质

Up to 4K resolution, 24/30/60 fps, HEVC encoding for efficient files. 最高 4K 分辨率，24/30/60 fps，HEVC 编码高效压缩。

05

Flexible Export 灵活导出

MOV for professional editing, MP4 for quick sharing. MOV 格式用于专业剪辑，MP4 用于快速分享。

06

Bilingual 双语支持

English and Simplified Chinese. Automatically adapts to your device. 英文和简体中文，自动跟随设备语言。
