Features 核心功能

Everything you need for professional dual-camera recording. 专业双摄录制所需的一切，尽在你的口袋中。

📹

Dual Simultaneous Recording 双摄同步录制

Record from both the wide-angle (portrait 9:16) and ultra-wide (landscape 16:9) cameras at the same time. 利用 AVCaptureMultiCamSession 技术，同时从广角和超广角摄像头录制视频。

🖥️

Split View Layout 分屏布局

See both camera feeds side by side in a vertically stacked split view. Full visibility of both perspectives. 上下分屏同时预览两个画面，超广角在上，广角在下，两个视角一目了然。

🪟

Picture-in-Picture Mode 画中画模式

Full-screen wide-angle preview with a draggable ultra-wide floating window. 广角全屏预览，超广角以可拖拽浮窗叠加显示，拖到任意位置获得最佳监看效果。

🎬

Pro Video Quality 专业视频质量

Choose between 1080p and 4K resolution, with frame rates of 24, 30, or 60 fps. 支持 1080p 和 4K 分辨率，帧率可选 24、30 或 60 fps，HEVC 编码高效控制文件大小。

📁

Flexible File Formats 灵活的文件格式

Export as MOV for professional editing, or choose MP4 for quick sharing on social media. 导出 MOV 格式可在 Final Cut Pro 中专业剪辑，选择 MP4 则可快速分享到社交媒体。

🌍

Bilingual Interface 双语界面

Full English and Simplified Chinese localization. Automatically adapts to your device language. 完整支持英文和简体中文，应用自动跟随设备语言设置进行切换。

Made For You 为你而造

🎥

Content Creators 内容创作者

Capture a close-up reaction and a wide establishing shot simultaneously. 同时拍摄特写反应和全景镜头，无需多台设备即可获得多角度素材。

📱

Vloggers 视频博主

Film yourself in portrait while recording the scene in ultra-wide landscape. 竖屏拍摄自己的同时，超广角横屏记录周围场景，一次拍摄获得 A-roll 和 B-roll。

🎓

Educators 教育工作者

Record yourself teaching while capturing the whiteboard or demonstration area. 录制教学画面的同时，用超广角捕捉白板或演示区域的全景。

✨

Everyday Users 普通用户

Record family events, travel moments, or anything where two perspectives tell a better story. 记录家庭活动、旅行瞬间，双视角让你的故事更加精彩。
