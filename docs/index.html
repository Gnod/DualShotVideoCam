Powerful Features 核心功能

Everything you need for professional dual-camera recording, right in your pocket. 专业双摄录制所需的一切，尽在你的口袋中。

📹

Dual Simultaneous Recording 双摄同步录制

Record from both the wide-angle (portrait 9:16) and ultra-wide (landscape 16:9) cameras at the same time using Apple's AVCaptureMultiCamSession technology. 利用 Apple AVCaptureMultiCamSession 技术，同时从广角（竖屏 9:16）和超广角（横屏 16:9）摄像头录制视频。

🖥️

Split View Layout 分屏布局

See both camera feeds side by side in a vertically stacked split view. Ultra-wide on top, wide on bottom — full visibility of both perspectives. 上下分屏同时预览两个画面，超广角在上，广角在下，两个视角一目了然。

🪟

Picture-in-Picture Mode 画中画模式

Full-screen wide-angle preview with a draggable ultra-wide floating window. Drag it anywhere on screen for the perfect monitoring position. 广角全屏预览，超广角以可拖拽浮窗叠加显示，拖到任意位置获得最佳监看效果。

🎬

Pro Video Quality 专业视频质量

Choose between 1080p and 4K resolution, with frame rates of 24, 30, or 60 fps. HEVC encoding keeps file sizes manageable without sacrificing quality. 支持 1080p 和 4K 分辨率，帧率可选 24、30 或 60 fps。HEVC 编码在保证画质的同时有效控制文件大小。

📁

Flexible File Formats 灵活的文件格式

Export as MOV for professional editing in Final Cut Pro or DaVinci Resolve, or choose MP4 for quick sharing on social media. 导出 MOV 格式可在 Final Cut Pro 或 DaVinci Resolve 中专业剪辑，选择 MP4 则可快速分享到社交媒体。

🌍

Bilingual Interface 双语界面

Full English and Simplified Chinese localization. The app automatically adapts to your device language setting. 完整支持英文和简体中文，应用会自动跟随您的设备语言设置进行切换。
